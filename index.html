Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Vlide Aviation's services, booking process, and more.

General Questions

Vlide Aviation is an urban air mobility company providing electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft services for passenger transportation and logistics solutions. We're revolutionizing urban transportation by offering fast, sustainable air travel above traffic congestion.

We currently operate in major metropolitan areas including San Francisco Bay Area, Los Angeles, and New York City. We're rapidly expanding to additional cities. Check our vertipad locations on the booking page for the most up-to-date service areas.

Our eVTOL aircraft are 100% electric with zero direct emissions during flight. They produce significantly less noise than helicopters and use renewable energy for charging whenever possible. We're committed to sustainability throughout our operations.

Booking & Pricing

You can book a flight through our website by visiting the "Book a Flight" page or through our mobile app. Simply select your departure and arrival locations, preferred date and time, service type, and complete the booking with your payment details.

Pricing varies based on distance, service type, and demand. Vlide Solo starts at $99, Vlide Group at $199, and Vlide Regional at $299. You can use our fare calculator during booking to get an exact quote for your specific route.

We offer free cancellation up to 2 hours before your scheduled departure. Cancellations within 2 hours of departure may be subject to a fee of up to 50% of the booking value. No-shows are charged the full booking amount.

Safety & Technology

Safety is our top priority. Our aircraft feature multiple redundancy systems, including backup power, propulsion, and navigation. They undergo rigorous testing and maintenance, and our pilots receive extensive training. We meet or exceed all regulatory safety requirements.

Like all aircraft, eVTOLs have weather limitations. We may cancel or reschedule flights during extreme weather conditions including heavy rain, high winds, lightning, or poor visibility. Safety always comes first, and we'll notify you as soon as possible if weather affects your flight.

Our aircraft are operated by professionally trained and certified pilots with extensive aviation experience. All pilots undergo rigorous training specific to our eVTOL aircraft and must meet strict safety and performance standards.

Services

Vlide Solo accommodates 1-2 passengers for short urban trips. Vlide Group carries 3-5 passengers for urban transportation. Vlide Regional offers extended range services for intercity travel up to 100 miles, accommodating 1-4 passengers depending on distance.

Vlide Solo allows one personal item (backpack or small bag) per passenger. Vlide Group allows one carry-on sized bag per passenger. Vlide Regional accommodates additional luggage depending on total passenger count. Weight restrictions apply for all services.

Vlide Cargo is our logistics service offering rapid package and freight delivery across urban areas. With a 200-pound capacity and point-to-point service, it's ideal for time-sensitive deliveries that need to bypass ground traffic congestion.
